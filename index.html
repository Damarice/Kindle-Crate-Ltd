About Kindle & Crate

Pioneering sustainable hospitality solutions

Our Story

Kindle & Crate is a premium hospitality supply company dedicated to providing sustainable fire and kitchen display solutions to hotels, restaurants, and retailers across Africa.

We represent eco-conscious brands and promote efficient energy use through quality design and environmental responsibility. Our commitment to sustainability goes hand-in-hand with our dedication to excellence.

Every product we offer is carefully selected to meet the highest standards of quality while minimizing environmental impact. From organic briquettes to professional-grade catering equipment, we supply the tools that power Africa's hospitality industry.

Our Mission

To deliver premium, eco-sustainable solutions that empower hospitality businesses to operate efficiently while protecting our planet for future generations.
